BzBert

An NLP model made specifically for the Algerian Local dialect, first part of the project was my graduation thesis were me and my friend made the larget sentiment analysis dataset, with a webapp to manually label extracted comments from algerian youtube channels.
The second part is the pretrained model, which will be tranined on about 12 million comments and replies extracted from Algerian youtube channels, the model was be trained using masked language modeling, and next sentence predition, and will very likely be open sourced soon.

WI-SAT

WI-SAT is small hotspot network that i build from scratch, it has a small real time QOE and QOS (qos and que gets updated based on few metrics) system that allows people to get the best experience out of their available bandwidth (or Wi-Fi airtime).
it leverages the power of Mikrotik and Ubiquiti, and it is my best computer network experience so far.
